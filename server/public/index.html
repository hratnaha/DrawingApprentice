Meet the Drawing Apprentice
A creative AI drawing partner that collaborates and learns from you! Drawing Apprentice AI was designed to improvise and collaborate on abstract sketches with users in real time. The system qualifies as a new genre of creative technologies termed “casual creators” that are meant to creatively engage users and provide enjoyable creative experiences rather than necessarily helping users make a higher quality creative product.
